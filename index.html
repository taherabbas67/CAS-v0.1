The Centre for Autonomous Systems is focused on the future of autonomous systems. It aims to do this by carrying out world-class research, providing high-quality training and education and collaborating with top companies in the industry to design, develop, enhance and analyse autonomous systems.


The Centre brings together students and faculties from across disciplines such as Engineering, Management and Science to work collaboratively on the next generation of autonomous systems such as robotics, autonomous underwater, ground and aerial vehicles and more!
